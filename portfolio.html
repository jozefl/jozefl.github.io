Coding

GCC Commits

  • My GCC contributions are focused on MSP430-specific improvements and bug fixes, including the implementation of an instruction cost model, and improvements to instruction selection.

Binutils/GDB Commits

  • My Binutils contributions include the implementation of the SHF_GNU_RETAIN ELF symbol flag and enhancements to MSP430 linker relocation processing.

Newlib Commits

  • My Newlib contributions include the implementation of a reduced code-size “printf” for MSP430, improvements to the MSP430 CRT library, and maintenance of libm.

MSP430-LLVM

  • This fork of LLVM is a staging area for MSP430-specific patches I developed, whilst they await review for inclusion into the main LLVM project.
  • The patches awaiting review can also be viewed on the LLVM Phabricator instance.

Technical Writing

ELF Extension - SHF_GNU_RETAIN - RFC/Specification

  • SHF_GNU_RETAIN is a new ELF symbol flag I designed and implemented in GNU Binutils. It was integrated upstream as a GNU extension to the ELF ABI, and facilitates the C/C++ “retain” attribute now available in GCC and LLVM/Clang.

MSP430-GCC User’s Guide

  • I enhanced many parts of the MSP430-GCC User’s Guide during my time working on the project.
  • I added “Section 5 - MSP430-GCC Features”, which describes the unique features of the toolchain in detail, and helps users make the most of the available optimizations.

ELF Extension - Symbol Meta-information - Proposal and ELF Implementation Details

  • I collaborated with Texas Instruments to adapt an ELF extension they had developed, so that it could be integrated into the upstream GNU toolchain.
  • This ELF extension wasn’t accepted upstream, but the whole process was a valuable learning experience, from writing the proposal and developing the technical specification, to engaging in the upstream mailing list discussions about the proposal.
  • I subsequently broke the specification into smaller parts, and upstreamed the functionality as separate features. SHF_GNU_RETAIN was one of those features.
